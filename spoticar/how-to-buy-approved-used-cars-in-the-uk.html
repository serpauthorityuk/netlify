How To Buy Approved Used Cars in the UK

When purchasing your next car, the initial question that usually comes to mind is whether to purchase a used car or a new one. Well, to help you make this difficult decision much easier, listed below are some of the benefits to choosing to buy an approved used car rather than a brand new one from the manufacturer’s factory.

The most commonly sold used cars for sale on the market at the moment are the second hand family SUV format, though used cars come in different shapes and sizes such as hatchbacks, estates, 4x4s, convertibles and more, as well as varying by models and features. For obvious reasons, second hand cars tend to be cheaper when compared to new ones, and more specifically it is because a new car will lose approximately 25% of its value in the first year, and up to 60% over the first 3 years.

It is important to think about the size, make, model and colour of the second hand cars that you are interested in to help you narrow your options. You can even go ahead and take a test drive of the pre-owned cars you are interested in so you can be more confident in your choice as this will allow you to know if the car you are interested in can match up to your expectations. Also check if there are proper maintenance records and repair history of the used cars for sale that you are interested in. This information will help you assess the condition of the pre-owned cars and what it would cost to restore it if the dealership has not already done so.

The price of second hand cars ranges from hundreds of pounds in the low range to thousands of pounds for the newer and more high end models. It is not only the brand and the model that matters, you also need to think about whether there are any special features you need and which trim level or model variant will be best for you and your family. Always make sure that you do not compromise on the quality of your next used car, and the best way to make sure you will be happy with your choice is to seek the advice of the staff at an approved used car garage.

There are several types of second hand cars on the market so you can easily choose the one that best suits your needs. You can choose from sedans, coupes, SUVs, vans, or hatchbacks among others. If you want to go in for a sporty SUV, you can choose something like a Range Rover Sport SVR, a Porsche Cayenne or a Seat Arona. If you prefer a small family hatchback then look into something like a Volkswagen Golf, or an approved used Peugeot. Whichever type of car you are interested in, it is important to choose the one that matches your expectations. For instance, if you are in need of a car with all wheel drive then make sure this is a feature the car has before you buy it.

It is possible to get great second hand cars at cheap prices from online sources like eBay or Gumtree. Online sources often offer cars at discounted prices because the sellers are private and the cars are usually sold without any form of warranty or guarantee. The second hand cars sold through these online sources often have some defects in their condition, whether cosmetic or mechanical. As such, you need to be careful while buying second hand cars through these sources.

In conclusion, most people prefer to buy used cars instead of new ones. However, this does not mean that they are not concerned about quality and there are many sources where you can find quality, affordable second hand cars. You can either buy a second hand car from a private party or visit a used car dealership. Most people are unable to pay for a used or new car outright and so finance options are a very attractive prospect. It is often best to source your finance with the dealership you are buying the car from, as personal bank loans can have higher interest rates and can even be secured against your existing property.
